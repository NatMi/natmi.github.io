Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

Brooklyn Bridge Park 5th - 8th August 2019






4 evenings with fantastic, amusing movies
★
Everyone is welcome

Join our bubbly summer festival in the heart of New York's famous location!

Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival and Brooklyn Bridge Park teamed up to bring the high quality open-air entertainment to reality.
Everyday from 5th to 8th August 2019 from 6pm until midnight, we will be showing some of the most amusing movies that will satisfy everyone.

The most exciting part? It's free! Nevertheless, due to limited venue space we need to ask you to reserve your free tickets.
Follow the link and join us on those warm evenings!
